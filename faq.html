FAQ

Everything you want to know about Poke Wave.

Home › FAQ
Where do you get your fish?
We source our ahi and salmon directly from the Honolulu Fish Auction on Nimitz Highway — the same place commercial fishing boats deliver their catch every morning. We pick up fresh fish daily before 6am, ensuring maximum freshness in every bowl.
Is your poke safe to eat raw?
Yes. We use sashimi-grade fish and maintain strict cold chain protocols from the auction to your bowl. All fish is kept below 38°F and served within hours of purchase. That said, pregnant individuals and immunocompromised people should consult their doctor before consuming raw fish.
Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?
Absolutely! Our Vegan Shore Bowl is 100% plant-based. Many of our other bowls are naturally gluten-free — just ask us to hold the wonton strips and use tamari instead of shoyu. We always have our full ingredient list available at the truck for allergy needs.
Where exactly is the truck parked?
On weekdays, we're at Kakaako Waterfront Park on Ala Moana Blvd — look for the blue truck near the main entrance. On Saturdays, we're at Ala Moana Center (mauka side). Sundays we're at the KCC Farmers Market. Follow us on Instagram @PokeWaveTruck for real-time location updates.
How does catering work?
We bring the truck to your event and serve fresh poke bowls to your guests. Packages start at 75 for up to 25 guests. We handle setup, service, and cleanup. Booking usually requires 2+ weeks advance notice for events under 50 people, and 4+ weeks for larger events.
Can I pre-order for pickup?
Yes! Call us at (808) 555-0107 or DM us on Instagram at least 30 minutes before your pickup to reserve your bowl. This ensures it's fresh and ready when you arrive without waiting in line.
What's your most popular bowl?
The Classic Ahi Wave and the Kakaako Crunch are neck-and-neck for #1. If it's your first time, we always recommend the Classic Ahi Wave — it's the most true-to-traditional poke experience and lets you taste the quality of our fish without distraction.
Do you offer a loyalty program?
Yes! Every 10th bowl is on us. Just ask for a punch card at the truck. We also offer a 10% discount for military, first responders, and teachers — just show your ID.
Do you serve alcoholic beverages?
No — we're a food-only truck. We do serve canned lilikoi lemonade, coconut water, and sparkling water. For catering events, clients are welcome to arrange their own bar service separately.
